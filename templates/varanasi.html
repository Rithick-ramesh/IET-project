About Varanasi

Varanasi is one of the oldest living cities in the world and one of the holiest places in India. Situated on the banks of the River Ganga, the city attracts millions of pilgrims and tourists every year.

Known for its spiritual atmosphere, ancient temples, evening Ganga Aarti, and sacred ghats, Varanasi offers a truly unforgettable cultural experience.

Best places to visit in Varanasi

Dashashwamedh Ghat

The most famous ghat in Varanasi, known for the spectacular evening Ganga Aarti.

Kashi Vishwanath Temple

One of the most sacred Hindu temples dedicated to Lord Shiva.

Sarnath

An important Buddhist pilgrimage site where Lord Buddha delivered his first sermon.
