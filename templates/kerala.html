About Kerala

Kerala is one of the most beautiful tourist destinations in India. Located on the southwestern coast, Kerala is famous for its lush greenery, serene backwaters, beautiful beaches, hill stations, and rich traditions.

The state offers unforgettable experiences such as houseboat cruises in Alleppey, tea gardens in Munnar, wildlife adventures in Thekkady, and relaxing Ayurvedic therapies.

Best places to visit in Kerala

Munnar

Famous for tea plantations, cool weather, waterfalls, and breathtaking mountain views.

Alleppey

Experience Kerala’s iconic backwaters with luxurious houseboat rides and peaceful scenery.

Thekkady

A wildlife paradise known for Periyar National Park, elephant rides, and spice plantations.
