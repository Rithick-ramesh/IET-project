About Kashmir

Kashmir is one of the most beautiful destinations in India, famous for its stunning valleys, snow-capped mountains, rivers, gardens, and natural beauty.

The region offers unforgettable experiences including shikara rides on Dal Lake, skiing in Gulmarg, trekking, and exploring traditional Kashmiri culture.

Best places to visit in Kashmir
