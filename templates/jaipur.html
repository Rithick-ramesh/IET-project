About Jaipur

Jaipur, the capital city of Rajasthan, is one of the most iconic tourist destinations in India. Famous for its pink-colored buildings, Jaipur beautifully blends history, culture, architecture, and traditions.

The city is home to magnificent attractions like Amber Fort, Hawa Mahal, City Palace, Jal Mahal, and Jantar Mantar. Jaipur is also known for traditional handicrafts, jewelry, and delicious Rajasthani cuisine.
